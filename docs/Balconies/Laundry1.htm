Basement Area

Dear Residents:

When using the Laundry facilities make sure you clean up after yourselves.

1. Clean out the Lint Basket when you are done drying.
2. Check machines to make sure you have all your clothes.
3. Throw all garbage in the trash, and not on the floor.
4. No eating, drinking, smoking or playing in the basement.
5. Do not leave laundry in the machine unattended or overnight.
6. The sink is specifically for the clothes and not to wash dishes.


Let’s try to keep our Laundry Room clean
